Projects

Teddy

This endearing Blender render features a plush teddy bear, a heartfelt creation crafted for a dear friend. Notably, the inclusion of a fur-modelling tool, employed for the first time, brings the bear's softness to life, enhancing its cuddly appeal. The meticulous attention to detail in the fur, combined with the adorable pose and expression, reflects both the joy of creation and the affection for the recipient. This rendering stands as a testament to my evolving skills, capturing the warmth and charm of a handmade gift created with care and creativity.

Candle

This early, successful rendering of a candle represents one of my initial forays into 3D design. The intricately crafted candle exhibits potential for further development, with a solid foundation for expanding the model into more complex creations. The play of light and shadow on the candle's surface adds a sense of realism, hinting at the possibilities for refining and leveraging this model for future projects. This rendering symbolizes the beginning of my creative journey in Blender, holding promise for further exploration and artistic growth.

Darth Vader

This amateur Darth Vader render proudly features my own creation. Despite its amateur status, I take pride in meticulous details. The red lightsaber casts an intense glow, reflecting off Vader's helmet and surroundings, adding a menacing aura. Subtle blue cosmic lights delicately touch Vader's mask, creating a captivating interplay of shadows. The juxtaposition of red and blue lighting intensifies the dramatic presence of Darth Vader, showcasing both my enthusiasm and dedication to capturing this iconic character.

Skull

This render represents a work-in-progress attempt to create a skull-like structure in Blender. While not yet achieving full realism, the model captures the essence of a human skull with its basic form and features. The unfinished nature of the render is intentional, showcasing the ongoing creative process. Textures are rudimentary, and certain details may be incomplete, highlighting the iterative nature of 3D modeling. This work offers a glimpse into the early stages of crafting a 3D skull model in Blender

Ball animation

This brief animation, crafted in Blender, showcases a meticulously designed sequence where a sphere gracefully navigates through openings in mirrors. The fluid motion of the sphere and the seamless interaction with the reflective surfaces demonstrate a high level of artistry and attention to detail. Each mirror strategically guides the sphere, creating a visually satisfying and harmonious passage. This animation stands as one of my recent and most refined works, highlighting my commitment to precision and creativity in Blender's artistic realm.
