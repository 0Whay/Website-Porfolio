3D Model Candle Render

This candle render features a single white pillar candle with a slightly textured surface. The flame of the candle is a bright, warm yellow, casting a soft glow on the surrounding area. 3D Model Teddy Bear Render

During my latest Blender project, I came across an unfamiliar tool that allowed me to achieve a realistic fur effect on a 3D model. The tool was called "Particle Hair" and it enabled me to add thousands of tiny hairs to the surface of the model, creating the appearance of fur.

3D Model Darth Vader Render

This Darth Vader render features the iconic Sith Lord standing tall against a dark, starry background. From the right side of the scene, a blue light shines down onto Vader, casting a cool glow across his helmet and armor. This lighting effect gives Vader an otherworldly and imposing appearance, as if he is illuminated by the cold, unforgiving light of the cosmos.
